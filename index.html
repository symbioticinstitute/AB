Integrated Board (CBSE) & AMU XI Entrance Success Program-2025

Class XI Entrance examination of Aligarh Muslim University is one of the toughest exams at this level. Every year around 25,000 students appear for roughly 1,500 seats, making the competition extremely fierce.

Key Features of Our Program

Our integrated approach ensures students build strong fundamentals early while preparing for both CBSE Board exams and the challenging AMU entrance test.

Early Foundation & Diagnostic

Begins well before board exams conclude, with diagnostic assessments of Class IX–X fundamentals to identify gaps early and build a strong foundation.

Integrated Syllabus Mapping

Systematic alignment of Class X revision modules (Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics or Social Studies) alongside CBSE Class X board preparation.

Progressive Entrance Prep Track

Regularly scheduled modules revisiting Class IX–X concepts in AMU-style objective format, with incremental difficulty and timed practice, ensuring no last-minute cramming.

Indo-Islamic Culture & GK Segment

Dedicated lessons and quizzes on Indo-Islamic Culture and general knowledge in AMU entrance style, delivered regularly to build cumulative familiarity.

Frequent Mock Tests & Analytics

Periodic full-length AMU-style mock exams under timed conditions; analytics dashboards highlight individual strengths and weaknesses, driving targeted remediation.
